Geo-Guardian Mobile App Privacy Policy (1/11/2024)

The Geo Guardian Mobile App (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive information about you. Any data processed by the App, such as QR code
